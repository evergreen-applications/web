Frequently asked

Why hasn't my package's status updated?

TrackPack checks for updates automatically in the background roughly every 30 minutes, but exact timing is controlled by iOS and isn't guaranteed. Pull down on the Packages list, or tap the refresh button, to check immediately.

TrackPack picked the wrong carrier — what do I do?

Open the package, tap Edit, and choose the correct carrier from the list. Status will refresh automatically once you save.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Open Settings on your iPhone → your name → Subscriptions → TrackPack, and cancel there. You can also tap "Manage Subscription" from within TrackPack's Settings tab.

How do I delete my data?

Delete the packages you no longer want tracked from within the app — this removes them from your iCloud account. Signing out locks the app behind Sign in with Apple again but doesn't delete anything.

Is TrackPack available on Android or the web?

Not currently — TrackPack is an iPhone app only, built around iOS features like Sign in with Apple, Live Activities, and iCloud sync.
